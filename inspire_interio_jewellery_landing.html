The Invisible Tax on Luxury Retail

Every square foot in a jewellery showroom does two jobs — sells trust, and sells desire. Most showrooms fail at both.

💎

Wrong Light, Dead Diamonds

Incorrect colour temperature makes yellow gold look brassy, white gold cold, diamonds smaller than their certificate. You lose ₹5L+ deals you never realize you had.

🔐

Fortress or Friendly

Visible bulky security, hostile counters, bank-like barriers make customers feel interrogated. First-time buyers leave before the consultation even begins.

🔊

No Acoustic Privacy

₹10L purchases need quiet conversations. When every customer overhears the negotiation, high-ticket closes collapse. Silence is the most underused design asset.

🧑‍💼

No Consultation Zones

Wedding shoppers need private, seated, unhurried consultation. Counter-only layouts push them to competitors with proper VIP rooms and family seating.
