The ₹10 Lakh Mistake Most Bangalore Homeowners Make

Choosing the cheapest quote is the most expensive decision you'll make on your new home.

🧩

40-Vendor Chaos

Modular from company A, false ceiling contractor B, painter C, electrician D, curtains E — nobody owns the final result. Every defect is someone else's fault. Every delay is a blame game.

⏰

Handover Delays

60-day promises become 5-month projects. You're paying apartment maintenance + rent + EMI while designers keep rescheduling. Every week costs you ₹20-30K in parallel living.

💸

Hidden Cost Escalation

Quote says ₹8L, final bill is ₹12L. "Extra charges" appear at every stage — electrical points, additional lights, hardware upgrades. Your budget bleeds silently.

🪵

Quality You Discover Later

Cheap plywood warps in monsoon year 2. Laminate peels in humid Bangalore. Modular joints sag. You're left with cosmetic fixes or full re-do in 4-5 years.
